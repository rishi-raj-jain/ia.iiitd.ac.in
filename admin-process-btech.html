Application Process For B.Tech

The Application process will be online.
There will be a nominal application processing fee of the order of USD 50. This may be revised from time to time.
The test scores should be submitted within the due date of the applicable round of admission.
After students submit the application, a one-2-one interaction with IIIT Delhi admissions committee may be arranged. This could be a 30 minute to 1 hr interaction in which the students will be assessed on key skills needed at IIIT Delhi (understanding English; Mathematics skills) .
