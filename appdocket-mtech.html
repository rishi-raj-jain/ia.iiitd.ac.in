Application Docket For M.Tech

Candidates are required to submit the following along with the application form:

● Transcripts of the program of study:
  • Certified by the institute of study,
  • Final Transcript to be submitted only by selected candidates to join the institute at the time of joining.

● A Statement of Purpose highlighting the following:
  • Which research group at IIIT appeals to the participant and why?
  • Key interests and hobbies and achievements there-in.
  • Describe the world you come from, for example, your college, club, community, city etc. How has this shaped your dreams and aspirations?
  • At IIIT Delhi we strive to make meaningful contributions to lives of others. How does your training till now equip you to do that?
  • Describe a key research problem that excites you and you would want to pursue in your post graduate studies.
  • What is the most significant challenge that you have faced in life, when things did not go according to plan. How did you manage it?

● Two (or preferably Three) Letters of Recommendation from faculty with whom the student has worked (done courses/ projects):
  • At least two of the recommendations from the faculty with whom the student has done projects and/ or courses.
  • Third recommendation should only be submitted if there are chances that it would highlight some other aspects of a student’s personality/ contribution.

● (Recommended) The student wcan share a portfolio.
  • research / maker project summary reports (max 10 pages)/ demo videos (max 2 minute duration);
  • 2-3 performance audios/ videos (max 2 minute duration each) highlighting one’s versatility;
  • 2-3 compositions etc (not more than 10 pages).

● In case of sponsored candidates, letter of sponsorship/ fellowship support wil be required.

● (Optional) Upto 4 activities beyond the regular curriculum can be presented with the challenges and achievements thereof.
